PRIVACY POLICY

INTRODUCTION

BEACON Pro is an educational ecosystem where training centers, teachers, parents, and students interact.

Our goal is to inform the student community about the existence of schools and training centers in Cameroon. In this app, schools showcase their available programs and tuition fees, allowing future students to make informed choices. Our regularly updated career dictionary helps students stay informed about developments in their field.

We are committed to being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your personal data with third parties.

The terms of this Privacy Policy apply as soon as you use our services (see Section 2).
