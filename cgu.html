TERMS OF USE

BEACON Pro is an educational ecosystem connecting training centers, teachers, parents, and students. Our goal is to link students with existing training centers in Cameroon. Through this platform, schools showcase their available programs and tuition fees to help prospective students make informed decisions. They will then gain the necessary skills for professional integration. Our regularly updated career dictionary will also keep students informed about trends in their field of study.
